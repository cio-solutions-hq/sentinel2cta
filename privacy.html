SMS Privacy Policy — CIO Sentinel Alerts

Last updated: June 2026

This SMS Privacy Policy describes how CIO Solutions ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with its opt-in SMS alerting program, CIO Sentinel Alerts. This policy supplements the general CIO Solutions privacy policy available at https://www.ciosolutions.com.

Information we collect

  • Mobile phone numbers that are provided to us by an authorized administrator at one of our managed-services customers, or that opt in via SMS keyword.
  • Inbound SMS replies from subscribers (e.g., YES, START, SUBSCRIBE, HELP, STOP).
  • Message delivery metadata (timestamp, delivery status) provided by our SMS carrier.

How we use this information

  • To send operational and infrastructure alert SMS messages (server outages, firewall outages, disk space warnings, service failures, backup failures, and similar incidents) to subscribers.
  • To process opt-in and opt-out requests.
  • To respond to HELP requests and provide customer support.
  • To maintain records of consent as required by the TCPA and carrier guidelines.

Consent

Subscribers opt in by texting YES, START, or SUBSCRIBE to the CIO Sentinel Alerts SMS number. Upon receipt, the system sends a confirmation message. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of any purchase or service. Subscribers may revoke consent at any time by replying STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, OPTOUT, or REVOKE.

Frequency and cost

Message frequency varies based on environment health and incident volume. Message and data rates may apply. CIO Solutions does not charge for the SMS service; standard carrier rates apply.

Mobile information and third parties

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Data retention

We retain mobile phone numbers and consent records for as long as the subscriber is enrolled in CIO Sentinel Alerts, plus a reasonable period thereafter to comply with our legal, audit, and recordkeeping obligations.

Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect subscriber information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.
