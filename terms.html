1. Program description

CIO Sentinel Alerts sends SMS messages to authorized IT and operations contacts at CIO Solutions managed-services customers. Alerts cover operational and infrastructure events, including server outages, firewall outages, disk space warnings, stopped Windows services, backup failures, and similar urgent incidents.

2. Opt-in

To subscribe, text YES, START, or SUBSCRIBE to the CIO Sentinel Alerts number. Upon receipt, you will receive a confirmation reply.
