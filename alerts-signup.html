CIO Sentinel Alerts — SMS Signup

CIO Solutions, a managed IT services provider headquartered in Santa Barbara, CA, offers an opt-in SMS alerting program called CIO Sentinel Alerts for authorized IT and operations contacts at our managed-services customers. Subscribers receive operational and infrastructure alerts about their environment, including:

  • Server unreachable / no-heartbeat notifications
  • Firewall down or offline notifications
  • Critical disk-space warnings
  • Stopped Windows services
  • Backup job failures
  • Other infrastructure issues requiring urgent attention

How to opt in

To subscribe to CIO Sentinel Alerts, text YES, START, or SUBSCRIBE to the CIO Sentinel Alerts SMS number provided to you by your CIO Solutions account team or technical contact.

Upon receipt, you will receive a confirmation reply:

"You have been subscribed to CIO Sentinel Alerts. Frequency varies. Msg&data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to cancel."

Program details

  • Program name: CIO Sentinel Alerts
  • Operated by: CIO Solutions (ciosolutions.com)
  • Message frequency: Varies based on environment health and incident volume.
  • Cost: Message and data rates may apply. Msg & data rates may apply.
  • Help: Reply HELP at any time, email support@ciosolutions.com, or call (805) 692-6700.
  • Opt out: Reply STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, OPTOUT, or REVOKE at any time. You will receive a confirmation: "You have successfully been unsubscribed. You will not receive any more messages from this number. Reply START to resubscribe."

Consent and privacy

By replying YES, START, or SUBSCRIBE, you consent to receive operational and infrastructure alert SMS messages from CIO Solutions at the mobile number from which the reply was sent. Consent to receive SMS messages is not required as a condition of purchase or service.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Sample messages

Examples of the alerts subscribers receive:

  • "CIO Sentinel Alert: Server [SERVERNAME] is down. No heartbeats detected for [N] minutes. A technician has been engaged. Reply HELP for support, STOP to opt out. Msg&data rates may apply."
  • "CIO Sentinel Alert: Spooler service stopped on [SERVERNAME] at [TIME]. Auto-remediation has been attempted. Reply HELP for support. Msg&data rates may apply."
  • "CIO Sentinel Alert: Low disk space on [SERVERNAME] — less than [N]% free space remaining on [VOLUME]. Immediate attention recommended. Reply HELP for support. Msg&data rates may apply."
  • "CIO Sentinel Alert: Firewall [DEVICENAME] at site [SITENAME] is unreachable. WAN heartbeat lost at [TIME]. Reply HELP for support, STOP to opt out. Msg&data rates may apply."
  • "CIO Sentinel Alert: Backup job [JOBNAME] failed on [SERVERNAME] at [TIME]. Error: [ERRORCODE]. Reply HELP for support. Msg&data rates may apply."
